About Us

Hello everyone, my name is Megha Chauhan, and I am a 2nd-year student pursuing a hotel management degree from Banarsidas Chandiwala College. My dream is to become a baker or a chef, and baking is my hobby. I decided to start my own cloud kitchen to accomplish my dream and refine my skills in baking. Baking is like an art to me, and people will only buy your art when it's aesthetically pleasing or eye-appealing. So, I ensure that whatever I bake is done with perfection to not disappoint my customers. It is challenging to manage studies and a cloud kitchen, but I spare some time to do so because this is what my dream is. My ultimate goal is to provide fresh-baked goods to my customers, as I've learned from hotel management that customers are our first priority.

Goal

My goal as a responsible home baker is to provide delicious bakery items at very reasonable and affordable prices, all while prioritizing hygiene. Baking is my passion, and I've transformed my passion into my career.
